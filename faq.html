FAQS

How do you charge for your services?

Our services are based on an hourly fee based on the complexity of the work being performed and the experience level of the staff performing the work. We feel we provide a great value when looking at the end quality of the work performed. The following services we provide are typically added free of charge because we believe in unparalleled customer service.

How can an accountant help a new business owner?

An accountant can help a new business owner in numerous ways. We can help with tax questions and preparations, we can help to set up payroll and employee benefits, or we can help with bookkeeping for businesses of any size. These are only a few of the services we can provide for entrepreneurs.

Do you provide other small business services, such as payroll accounting?

Yes, Accuracy provides payroll accounting for any size of business. We understand that each business is unique, and we provide services customized to each client’s specific needs.

Can Accuracy help me with my QuickBooks or other accounting software?

Yes, Accuracy provides support for the QuickBooks software package. With QuickBooks Certified ProAdvisors, we can provide troubleshooting as well as straining sessions to help you use Quickbooks more efficiently and maximize all the benefits it provides.

What hours are you available?

While our typical office hours are 8am to 5pm, Monday through Friday. Special arrangements for early morning, evening, or weekend consultations can also be made.

Can you E-file taxes for me or my business?

YES! We are an authorized e-file provider and strive to efile as many returns as we reasonably can.
