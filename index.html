Company Overview

Accuracy Accounting was founded in 2001 by Seward’s own Austin Dobrusky. Dobrusky and his staff are currently using their vast knowledge of the business and financial world to offer accounting services to all forms of clients both in the Seward area and worldwide via the internet. These accounting services will be tailored to meet each client’s individual needs in any market by combining traditional skills with the latest technologies and strategies to deliver solutions. Accuracy currently has a staff of over 25 highly respected employees, who are here to help you or your business in any way possible. While we are headquartered in beautiful downtown Seward, Nebraska, our services can be utilized almost anywhere in the world through our online capabilities.
